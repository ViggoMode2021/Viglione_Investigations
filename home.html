Thank you for visiting Viglione's Private Investigations

Viglione Investigations  is your preferred private investigator serving all of CT. Sal Viglione is a retired police officer with the New Haven police department who oversees and assist clients to win their cases. As licensed investigators, we respect your need for a private investigator and handle your case with the utmost care and discretion.  With over 25 years of experience  Salvatore Viglione has the insight for what happens in proceedings and what effects sloppy police work can have on a falsely accused perpetrator.  
Viglione Investigations specializes in criminal defense cases specializing in habeas corpus, indigent, court appointed, conflict and ex parte cases. Salvatore Viglione is a vendor for the Connecticut Public Defenders office. We also handle all types of business and personal fraud and litigation services, but we are well suited to handle any type of investigation from simple surveillance investigations to more complex fraud and embezzlement cases.

We are  very meticulous with all our services and if the client qualifies, the state may pay for their services and pay the fees of the attorney and the investigator (although private cases require a retainer). Expert witness statements, photographs, and videos can be taken to help solidify and win your case!
